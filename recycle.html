Total Recorded Waste vs. Total Recycled Waste, 2016 - 2019

The area chart above displays the total recorded amount of waste recorded in relation to the total amount of waste recycled. Fate of Recorded Waste, 2018 - 2019

The pie chart above illustrates the various fates of recorded waste during the 2018-2019 period. As displayed, Recycling (highlighted in green) accounts for almost two thirds of all recorded waste, followed by landfill which nearly consist of the remaining third. Other output streams and their exact amount of waste can be seen by hovering over each segment.

Source: Australian Bureau of Statistics
